Super Simple Banana Ice Cream

This super easy Banana Ice Cream is a great way to cool off on a hot day with only a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup full fat coconut milk
Possible Add Ins:
  • chocolate chips
  • peanut butter
  • strawberries or other fruit
  • almonds or other nuts/seeds

Steps

  1. blend frozen bananas with coconut milk, long enough to blend but not too long so the bananas don't heat up too much
  2. add any desired add ins
  3. put into container and freeze for at least 1 hour
  4. enjoy!
