Peanut Fried Rice

Easy to make Peanut Fried Rice with Tofu

This recipe has a blend of tamari, garlic peanut sauce with baked tofu a few veggies and filled out with some hearty brown rice.

It is basically a one pan dish other than baking the tofu in the toaster or oven.

Ingredients

For Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • dash of chili garlice sauce
For Baked Tofu:
  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp tamari
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
For Veggie Fried Rice:
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 small white onion chopped
  • 2 medium carrots chopped
  • 1 cup brown rice

Steps

  1. add 1 cup brown rice to rice cooker with 2 cups water and start
  2. chop onion and add to pan with a little oil
  3. preheat toaster to 350 degrees F
  4. as onion cooks chop tofu and mix with tamari, oil and cornstarch and add to toaster oven on some parchment paper
  5. add broccoli and carrot to onion
  6. mix all ingredients of the sauce
  7. once rice is finished mix with sauteed veggies and add the sauce
  8. top with baked tofu
